# MEDACCER — Product Brief

## What it is
MEDACCER is an AI WhatsApp assistant for independent healthcare clinics in Colombia. It connects to the clinic's WhatsApp Business number and Google Calendar, automating patient scheduling 24/7 without the doctor or staff needing to touch their phone.

## Target users
**Primary:** Independent healthcare professionals — general medicine, dentistry, dermatology, psychology, physiotherapy, nutrition, aesthetic medicine, traumatology, pediatrics. 1–4 practitioners, no dedicated admin staff, located in Colombian cities.

**Secondary:** Small clinic managers who handle scheduling manually across 2–5 practitioners.

## Core pain being solved
A healthcare worker spends ~26 hours/week answering WhatsApp messages manually: scheduling appointments, confirming, rescheduling, sending reminders. They cannot attend patients and answer chats at the same time. MEDACCER eliminates this manually repetitive loop without requiring new software or data migrations.

## Key differentiators
- WhatsApp-native (95% open rate in LATAM vs. 22% email)
- Specialty-aware vocabulary and pricing suggestions (9 specialties out of the box)
- Compliant with Resolución 1888/2025 (Colombia) — generates RDA automatically
- HL7 FHIR native
- 48h setup, no code, no hardware
- Integrates with Google Calendar (what practitioners already use)
- Voice-to-text clinical notes with AI structuring

## Pricing (COP/month)
- Starter: $129,000/month — 1 practitioner, up to 200 monthly appointments
- Pro: $229,000/month — up to 3 practitioners, unlimited appointments, marketing campaigns
- Clínica: $349,000/month — up to 10 practitioners, white-label bot, API access

## Brand tone
Direct, warm, no-nonsense. Speaks to busy health professionals, not engineers. Uses plain Spanish (no anglicisms). Conveys trust and clinical seriousness without being stiff. Avoids startup buzzwords. 

## Anti-references (never look like this)
- Generic SaaS landing pages with blue gradients and stock photos of laptops
- Medical software that looks like a hospital ERP (cold, grey, corporate)
- Chatbot marketing with robot emojis and "AI-powered" in every sentence
- Pricing pages with confusing feature matrices

## Strategic principles
1. Show the product working — don't just describe features
2. Lead with time saved, not technology used
3. Compliance (Res. 1888) is a trust signal, not a burden
4. Early adopter program for first 50 clinics: price locked for life + 1:1 support
